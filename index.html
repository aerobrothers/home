Services

Car lineup

Nissan 350Z (Z33)

Brakes are hot and fading at track?

Keep them cool and reduce brake consumption all at once! These are brake cooling deflectors made for the 350z/G35 COUPE chassis (03-06 RWD sedans) These divert air from under the car and the wheel well towards the tiny brake cooling inlet the Nissan engineers so graciously left us. You can see the tiny inlet circled in red there. Yup, they hid it behind there. You need high pressure there to get the most out your rotor's cooling vanes. These deflectors are how you get it.

These drastically reduced running temperature on track and the street for me, I cannot say exactly how much it will lower your braking temperatures as all tracks/roads/climates are a bit different and everyone is running different pads. For me it was nearly 300F. Every Porsche RS model comes with these standard as well as Hyundai N models, AMGs, some Ferraris, and if you buy a new Z06 you'll get a big bag of these in the frunk you are meant to add to the car if you do track days.!
