Services

We are an automotive aerodynamic consulting service and aerodynamic parts manufacturer located in Chicago ,IL. our team is led by a track racing expert and an aerodynamicist mechanical engineer specialized in CFD simulations. Our 3D CAD models are highly accurate based on scans of real cars and optimized for maximum accuracy and fitment for our parts. We offer aerodynamic consulting services as we scan your car and evaluate your existing or potential aerodynamic modifications and setup, please don't hesitate to contact us for an appointment. We also offer readily available functional aerodynamic parts that are validated with CFD.

CAR Taylored and CFD verified aerodynamic parts

WE offer functional aerodynamic parts and accessories taylored specifically for each car in the lineup, parts are designed and engineered using state of the art industrial grade 3D scanning and simulated using very accurate computatuinal simulation models, to insure both perfect fitment and accurate simulation.

Universal CFD verified aerodynamic parts

We also offer universal aerodynamic parts and accessories that are designed and engineered computatuinal simulation models, to insure proof of concept for a very wide range of applications, including but not limited to endplates for common wings, and front splitter diffusers.

Car products

Nissan 350Z (Z33)

Brakes are hot and fading at track?

Keep them cool and reduce brake consumption all at once! These are brake cooling deflectors made for the 350z/G35 COUPE chassis (03-06 RWD sedans) These divert air from under the car and the wheel well towards the tiny brake cooling inlet the Nissan engineers so graciously left us. You can see the tiny inlet circled in red there. Yup, they hid it behind there. You need high pressure there to get the most out your rotor's cooling vanes. These deflectors are how you get it.

These drastically reduced running temperature on track and the street for me, I cannot say exactly how much it will lower your braking temperatures as all tracks/roads/climates are a bit different and everyone is running different pads. For me it was nearly 300F. Every Porsche RS model comes with these standard as well as Hyundai N models, AMGs, some Ferraris, and if you buy a new Z06 you'll get a big bag of these in the frunk you are meant to add to the car if you do track days.!

Porsche Brembo 16Z rear caliper adapters

The Brembo 16z calipers offers an excellent upgrade for the rear brakes from the stock Brembo 2 piston calipers, the 4 piston design has better heat managment on street and track while also offering better brake balance when the fronts are equipped with a 6 piston upgrade.
